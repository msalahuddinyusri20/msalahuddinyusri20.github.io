I am **Muhammad Salahuddin**, a 2025 SPM Graduate dedicated to academic excellence and leadership development. This site is a comprehensive website of my journey, designed to provide a deeper look into the achievements and experiences beyond my CV.

## My Vision

Malaysia stands at a crossroads, rich in energy resources, yet still dependent on foreign 
technology to extract, conserve, and sustain them. I want to be the engineer who changes that.

My goal is to pursue **Mechanical Engineering** with a focus on **sustainable energy systems** by designing and innovating technologies that reduce that dependency, improve energy efficiency, 
and put Malaysian-built solutions on the world stage alongside technology leading nations like Germany and China. Every discipline I have built, the precision of KRS drill, the analytical skills of competitive 
mathematics, and the memorisation discipline of Tahfiz Al-Quran has been preparing me for the demands of 
engineering at that level.

This is not just a career path. It is a mission.
